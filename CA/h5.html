These hotels near Ekana Stadium provide various options for travelers looking for luxury, comfort, and convenience while visiting Lucknow. here are five top hotels that offer comfort, convenience, and a variety of amenities:



1. Hyatt Regency Lucknow

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 7 km

  • Highlights: Located in Gomti Nagar, Hyatt Regency offers modern, spacious rooms, multiple dining options, a luxurious spa, and an outdoor pool. Known for its excellent service and central location, this hotel is ideal for both business and leisure travelers.

  • Ideal for: Those looking for a luxurious, comfortable stay with easy access to Lucknow’s major attractions.

2. Renaissance Lucknow Hotel

  • Distance from Stadium: About 8 km

  • Highlights: This 5-star property offers stunning views of the Gomti River, with modern rooms, a rooftop infinity pool, and several dining options. The rooftop bar, Sky Bar, is a highlight, providing guests with panoramic views of the city.

  • Ideal for: Luxury seekers and those wanting scenic views and top-notch amenities.

3. Fairfield by Marriott Lucknow

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 8 km
  • Highlights: Also situated in Gomti Nagar, Fairfield offers contemporary rooms, a well-equipped fitness center, and an in-house restaurant serving a mix of Indian and international cuisine. Its modern design and affordable luxury make it a popular choice.
  • Ideal for: Business travelers and tourists looking for a stylish, comfortable stay at a reasonable price.

4. Novotel Lucknow Gomti Nagar

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 6 km

  • Highlights: With chic interiors, an outdoor pool, fitness center, and multiple dining options, Novotel offers both comfort and convenience. It’s well-suited for families and business travelers alike, with spacious rooms and kid-friendly amenities.

  • Ideal for: Families, business travelers, and those wanting a balanced mix of leisure and convenience.

5. Hilton Garden Inn Lucknow

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 7 km

  • Highlights: Located in the heart of Gomti Nagar, Hilton Garden Inn features contemporary rooms, an outdoor pool, a well-equipped gym, and a 24-hour pantry. The hotel’s all-day dining restaurant serves both Indian and international dishes, making it a great option for dining variety.

  • Ideal for: Tourists and business travelers seeking modern amenities and easy access to Lucknow’s popular spots.
