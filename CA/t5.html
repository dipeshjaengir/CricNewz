If you're visiting Ekana Cricket Stadium (BRSABV Ekana Stadium) in Lucknow, there are several notable attractions nearby that showcase the city’s rich history, vibrant culture, and architectural beauty.These attractions near Ekana Stadium offer a rich blend of history, culture, and leisure, making them ideal for visitors who want to experience the essence of Lucknow beyond the cricket matches. Here are five top tourist places to explore near the stadium:



1. Bara Imambara

  • Distance from Stadium: About 10 km

  • Highlights: Built in 1784 by Nawab Asaf-ud-Daula, Bara Imambara is a grand complex known for its labyrinthine architecture and stunning central hall, which is one of the largest arched constructions without support beams. The “Bhool Bhulaiya” (maze) within the structure is a highlight, attracting tourists and history buffs alike. The surrounding gardens and mosque add to the historic charm of the site.

  • Ideal for: History enthusiasts, architecture lovers, and families.

2.Chota Imambara

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 12 km

  • Highlights: Known as the "Palace of Lights," Chota Imambara is an exquisite architectural marvel built by Nawab Muhammad Ali Shah in 1838. The interior is adorned with chandeliers, calligraphy, and intricate artwork, and the complex also includes the Nawab’s tomb. Its beautiful setting and reflection in the surrounding water make it a stunning spot for photography.

  • Ideal for: Art lovers, history aficionados, and those interested in cultural sites.

3. Rumi Darwaza

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 10 km

  • Highlights: This 18th-century gateway is one of Lucknow’s most iconic landmarks, built by Nawab Asaf-ud-Daula. Standing 60 feet tall, Rumi Darwaza is known for its Mughal-inspired design, including intricate floral motifs and impressive arches. It’s a popular spot for photography and a must-visit for those exploring the city’s architectural heritage.

  • Ideal for: Architecture enthusiasts and photographers.

4. Hazratganj Market

  • Distance from Stadium: About 10 km

  • Highlights: Hazratganj is Lucknow’s bustling shopping and entertainment district, known for its mix of old-world charm and modern amenities. With an array of shops, boutiques, cafes, and restaurants, it’s the perfect spot for shopping, dining, and experiencing Lucknow’s vibrant lifestyle. The market is especially beautiful in the evening when it’s illuminated.

  • Ideal for: Shoppers, food lovers, and those looking to explore Lucknow’s lively culture.

5. Ambedkar Memorial Park

  • Distance from Stadium: 6 km

  • Highlights: Spanning over 100 acres, Ambedkar Memorial Park is a massive architectural complex dedicated to Dr. B.R. Ambedkar and other social reformers. The park features grand structures, sandstone sculptures, lush green lawns, and beautiful fountains. The sprawling grounds and serene atmosphere make it a peaceful place for an evening walk or relaxation.

  • Ideal for: Families, history lovers, and those looking for a tranquil experience.
