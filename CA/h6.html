These hotels provide a range of options for different preferences, from luxury and family-friendly resorts to centrally located accommodations, all close to HPCA Stadium and the attractions of Dharamshala.Here are five top-rated hotels nearby that offer comfort, scenic views, and convenient access to the stadium and surrounding attractions:



1. Fortune Park Moksha

  • Distance from Stadium: About 7 km

  • Highlights: Situated in the scenic Strawberry Hills, Fortune Park Moksha offers luxurious rooms with stunning views of the Dhauladhar range. With a spa, outdoor pool, and multiple dining options, this hotel provides a relaxing mountain retreat experience.

  • Ideal for: Travelers seeking a luxurious stay with mountain views and resort-style amenities.

2. Hotel Inclover

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 4 km

  • Highlights: Located in the heart of Dharamshala, this hotel offers modern rooms with beautiful valley views. It has an in-house restaurant, and its convenient location allows easy access to the stadium and nearby attractions.

  • Ideal for: Those looking for comfort and accessibility in the town center.

3. Hotel Dhauladhar by HPTDC

  • Distance from Stadium: About 4 km

  • Highlights: Managed by Himachal Pradesh Tourism, Hotel Dhauladhar offers cozy rooms with traditional decor and panoramic views of the Dhauladhar mountains. Its proximity to the market and local attractions makes it a convenient choice.

  • Ideal for: Budget-conscious travelers and those looking for an authentic Himachali experience.

4. Club Mahindra Dharamshala Resort

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 7 km

  • Highlights: This resort offers comfortable accommodations, a children’s play area, a game room, and an on-site restaurant serving local and international cuisine. Its tranquil setting makes it ideal for a relaxing getaway in nature.

  • Ideal for: Families and leisure travelers looking for a relaxing retreat with activities.

5. The Divine Hima

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 5 km

  • Highlights: This boutique hotel is known for its artistic decor, personalized service, and peaceful setting. It has beautifully designed rooms, a library, a gallery, and an in-house café with mountain views.

  • Ideal for: Art enthusiasts and those looking for a unique, serene experience in a boutique setting.
