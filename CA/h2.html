1.Taj Connemara, Chennai

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 3 km

  • Highlights: Taj Connemara is Chennai's oldest luxury hotel, blending classic colonial architecture with modern amenities. It offers spacious rooms, a beautiful pool, a well-equipped fitness center, and multiple dining options, including local South Indian cuisine. Known for its warm hospitality and historic charm, this hotel is a favorite among both leisure and business travelers.

  • Ideal for: Those seeking a luxurious, heritage-rich experience in the heart of the city.

2.The Leela Palace Chennai

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 6 km

  • Highlights: Located along the scenic Bay of Bengal, The Leela Palace offers stunning sea views and opulent decor. The hotel features an infinity pool, a full-service spa, fine dining restaurants, and a variety of luxurious room options. Its coastal location and elegant ambiance make it one of Chennai’s most sought-after hotels.

  • Ideal for: Travelers looking for a beachfront luxury experience with top-notch amenities.

3.Hyatt Regency Chennai

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 5 km

  • Highlights: This contemporary hotel is known for its stylish rooms, excellent service, and modern amenities. It offers multiple dining options, including a famous all-day buffet, a rooftop bar, and an extensive spa. The Hyatt Regency is also close to key shopping and business areas, making it convenient for exploring the city.

  • Ideal for: Business travelers and tourists seeking modern luxury and proximity to city attractions.

4.Radisson Blu Hotel Chennai City Centre

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 4 km

  • Highlights: Radisson Blu offers modern rooms, excellent dining, and a range of amenities, including a spa, outdoor pool, and fitness center. With its chic ambiance, it’s popular among both business and leisure travelers. The hotel’s central location provides easy access to both the stadium and various cultural attractions in Chennai.

  • Ideal for: Travelers looking for a comfortable stay with easy access to city landmarks and shopping areas.

5.The Park Chennai

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 4 km

  • Highlights: This trendy boutique hotel offers a blend of comfort and style with a touch of luxury. Known for its vibrant interiors and rooftop pool, The Park also features an upscale restaurant and bar with live entertainment. It’s conveniently located near shopping hubs and cultural sites.

  • Ideal for: Travelers who enjoy a lively atmosphere with modern amenities and easy access to Chennai’s nightlife.
