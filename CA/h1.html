Each of these hotels offers a unique experience and provides easy access to the Narendra Modi Stadium, as well as other key sights and attractions in Ahmedabad. Whether you’re looking for luxury or budget-friendly options, these hotels cater to diverse needs for a comfortable stay.

Here are five top-rated hotels near the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad, providing comfort, convenience, and a range of amenities for travelers visiting the city:



1. Hyatt Regency Ahmedabad

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 7 km

  • Highlights: This luxury hotel is known for its contemporary design, spacious rooms, and excellent hospitality. It offers a variety of dining options, including Indian and international cuisines, and amenities such as an outdoor pool, spa, and fitness center. The hotel is also close to Sabarmati Riverfront, providing scenic views and easy access to popular attractions.

  • Ideal for: Business travelers, families, and tourists looking for a luxurious stay with proximity to key city sites.

2. Courtyard by Marriott Ahmedabad

  • Distance from Stadium: About 13 km

  • Highlights: Courtyard by Marriott offers modern accommodations with well-appointed rooms and suites, multiple dining options, and extensive business facilities. Amenities include an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a spa. Known for its attentive service, this hotel is popular among business travelers and tourists alike.

  • Ideal for: Travelers seeking modern amenities, business facilities, and a comfortable stay with Marriott's signature hospitality.

3. The Ummed Ahmedabad

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 6 km

  • Highlights: A classic five-star hotel with an old-world charm, The Ummed features lush gardens, an outdoor pool, and elegantly designed rooms. With a focus on traditional Indian hospitality, the hotel also offers multiple dining options, including local and continental cuisines.

  • Ideal for: Travelers who prefer a luxurious stay with a traditional feel and close proximity to the airport and stadium.

4. Holiday Inn Express Ahmedabad Ashram Road

  • Distance from Stadium: About 7 km

  • Highlights: This hotel provides clean, comfortable, and affordable accommodations with modern amenities, including free breakfast, Wi-Fi, and fitness facilities. It’s a good choice for travelers looking for a value-for-money stay with easy access to the stadium and other attractions, like Sabarmati Ashram and the Riverfront.

  • Ideal for: Budget-conscious travelers, solo travelers, and business travelers looking for convenience and comfort.

5. Lemon Tree Premier The Atrium

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 9 km

  • Highlights: This upscale hotel features spacious rooms with modern decor, an indoor pool, and multiple dining options. Located near the Sabarmati River, it offers beautiful views and easy access to nearby attractions. Lemon Tree Premier is known for its pleasant ambiance and friendly service.

  • Ideal for: Couples, families, and business travelers looking for a peaceful yet luxurious stay near central Ahmedabad.
