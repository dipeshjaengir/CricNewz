1. The Ritz-Carlton, Bangalore

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 1.5 km

  • Highlights: Known for its opulence and exceptional service, The Ritz-Carlton features elegant rooms, a luxurious spa, and multiple fine-dining options, including a rooftop bar with stunning city views. The hotel’s design blends traditional Indian elements with contemporary style, offering a tranquil oasis in the heart of the city.
  • Ideal for: Travelers seeking a high-end, luxurious experience.

2. ITC Gardenia, a Luxury Collection Hotel

  • Distance from Stadium: About 2 km
  • Highlights: ITC Gardenia combines luxury with sustainability and is known for its eco-friendly practices. The hotel offers spacious rooms, multiple dining options, a wellness spa, and an outdoor pool. With its lush, garden-inspired design, it provides a serene retreat from the city’s hustle.
  • Ideal for: Eco-conscious travelers looking for luxury and relaxation.

3. St. Mark's Hotel

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 1 km

  • Highlights: St. Mark’s Hotel is a popular choice for its warm hospitality, comfortable rooms, and excellent location. The hotel has an in-house restaurant and bar, along with modern amenities, making it ideal for both business and leisure travelers. Its proximity to M.G. Road and UB City adds to its convenience.

  • Ideal for: Business travelers and tourists looking for comfort and convenience.

4. JW Marriott Hotel Bengaluru

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 1 km

  • Highlights: Located near Cubbon Park, JW Marriott offers spacious rooms with park or city views, a luxurious spa, and multiple dining options. The hotel’s outdoor pool, modern fitness center, and business facilities make it a great option for a well-rounded stay.

  • Ideal for: Families, business travelers, and leisure travelers who value premium amenities.

5. Taj MG Road, Bengaluru

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 2 km

  • Highlights: Known for its blend of classic luxury and modern amenities, Taj MG Road features well-appointed rooms, a pool, a spa, and an excellent selection of dining options, including an award-winning restaurant with international and Indian cuisine. Its central location on M.G. Road offers easy access to shopping, dining, and entertainment.

  • Ideal for: Those seeking a luxurious and centrally-located stay with Taj’s renowned hospitality.
