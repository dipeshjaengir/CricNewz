If you’re visiting Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai and looking for comfortable accommodations nearby, here are some of the best hotels in the area, offering a range of amenities and close proximity to the stadium and other attractions in South Mumbai:



1. The Oberoi, Mumbai

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 2 km

  • Highlights: Known for its luxury, The Oberoi offers elegantly designed rooms with views of the Arabian Sea, excellent dining options, a spa, and an outdoor pool. The hotel is known for its impeccable service and modern amenities, making it a top choice for both leisure and business travelers.

  • Ideal for: Those seeking a luxurious stay with world-class facilities and sea views.

2. Trident, Nariman Point

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 2 km

  • Highlights: Another high-end option, Trident Nariman Point offers stunning views of Marine Drive and the Arabian Sea. The hotel has spacious rooms, a fitness center, an outdoor pool, and several dining options that include Indian and international cuisines. Its central location allows easy access to many tourist attractions.

  • Ideal for: Business travelers and tourists looking for comfort, convenience, and scenic views.

3.InterContinental Marine Drive

  • Distance from Stadium: 1 km

  • Highlights: Located right on Marine Drive, this boutique luxury hotel offers spacious, well-furnished rooms with sea views. The hotel has an impressive rooftop pool and bar, as well as a fine-dining restaurant that offers Indian and international cuisines.

  • Ideal for: Travelers who enjoy boutique luxury with modern amenities and a sea view.

4. Hotel Marine Plaza

  • Distance from Stadium: About 1 km

  • Highlights: Hotel Marine Plaza offers stylish accommodations with views of the Arabian Sea and Marine Drive. The hotel features an outdoor pool, a rooftop terrace, and multiple dining options, including a popular buffet. It’s known for its comfortable rooms, warm hospitality, and convenient location.

  • Ideal for: Leisure travelers, families, and couples looking for comfortable, mid-range accommodations near Marine Drive.

5.The Ambassador, Mumbai

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 1 km

  • Highlights: This centrally located hotel has a vintage charm with modern amenities, including well-appointed rooms, a fitness center, and a restaurant that offers a variety of cuisines. Known for its iconic revolving restaurant, The Pearl of the Orient, the Ambassador is a unique experience in itself.

  • Ideal for: Travelers looking for a centrally located, mid-range hotel with a touch of classic charm.
