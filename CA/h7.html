Here are five top-rated hotels near the MCA Stadium in Pune, offering comfort, convenience, and a variety of amenities:



1. Courtyard by Marriott Pune Chakan

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 12 km

  • Highlights: This hotel is known for its modern rooms, an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and multiple dining options. The hotel also offers easy access to the Pune-Chakan industrial belt, making it popular among business travelers.

  • Ideal for: Business and leisure travelers looking for a comfortable stay with upscale amenities.

2. Holiday Inn Express Pune Pimpri

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 18 km

  • Highlights: Known for its clean, modern rooms, Holiday Inn Express provides complimentary breakfast, a fitness center, and convenient access to nearby areas. It’s a great option for travelers looking for a simple, comfortable stay with essential amenities.

  • Ideal for: Budget-conscious travelers and business guests seeking a reliable hotel option.

3. Sayaji Hotel Pune

  • Distance from Stadium: About 19 km

  • Highlights: Sayaji Hotel offers luxury with spacious rooms, multiple dining options, and a rooftop pool. It also features a fitness center and spa services, making it a comfortable and enjoyable stay.

  • Ideal for: Travelers looking for a blend of luxury, relaxation, and entertainment.

4. The Orchid Hotel Pune

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 16 km

  • Highlights: This eco-friendly luxury hotel offers elegant rooms, an outdoor pool, and a variety of dining options. Its scenic location on the Mumbai-Pune Bypass makes it convenient for those visiting both Pune and nearby areas.

  • Ideal for: Families and leisure travelers looking for a comfortable stay with eco-friendly practices.

5. DoubleTree by Hilton Pune-Chinchwad

  • Distance from Stadium: About 20 km
  • Highlights: DoubleTree by Hilton offers comfortable, stylish rooms, a fitness center, rooftop dining, and a swimming pool. Known for its exceptional service, this hotel provides a luxurious yet convenient experience.

  • Ideal for: Business travelers and families seeking high-end amenities and easy access to Pune’s major attractions.
