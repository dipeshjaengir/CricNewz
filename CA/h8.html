These hotels near the Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium offer a range of options from luxury to convenience, catering to a variety of travelers visiting Hyderabad.
Here are five recommended hotels near the Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium in Hyderabad, offering comfort, convenience, and various amenities:



1. Hyatt Place Hyderabad Banjara Hills

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 18 km

  • Highlights: This modern hotel offers spacious rooms, a rooftop pool, a fitness center, and an on-site restaurant serving a mix of local and international cuisine. Hyatt Place is known for its excellent service and convenient location in one of Hyderabad’s bustling areas.

  • Ideal for: Business travelers and tourists seeking a comfortable stay with modern amenities.

2.ITC Kohenur, a Luxury Collection Hotel

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 21 km

  • Highlights: This luxury hotel in HITEC City features opulent rooms, multiple dining options, a spa, and a scenic pool with views of the city. The hotel combines luxury and a touch of Hyderabad’s heritage in its design and décor.

  • Ideal for: Luxury travelers looking for a high-end stay with top-notch services.

3. Novotel Hyderabad Airport

  • Distance from Stadium: Approximately 23 km

  • Highlights: Located near Rajiv Gandhi International Airport, Novotel offers spacious rooms, an outdoor pool, fitness facilities, and multiple dining options. With a relaxed ambiance, it’s an excellent option for travelers with flights to catch.

  • Ideal for: Travelers looking for a quick commute to the airport and those seeking a relaxing layover.

4. The Park Hyderabad

  • Distance from Stadium: About 18 km

  • Highlights: This luxury hotel overlooking Hussain Sagar Lake offers chic, stylish rooms, a spa, and a lively nightclub. Its central location provides easy access to nearby attractions, and the stunning views make it a popular choice.

  • Ideal for: Young travelers and those looking for nightlife options along with luxury amenities.

5. Taj Deccan

  • Distance from Stadium: Around 17 km

  • Highlights: Located in Banjara Hills, this hotel combines traditional charm with modern comforts. It features well-appointed rooms, lush gardens, a pool, and several dining choices. Known for its impeccable service and serene ambiance, it’s a popular choice among both business and leisure travelers.

  • Ideal for: Those seeking a blend of traditional elegance and modern amenities.
